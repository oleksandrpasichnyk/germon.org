OUR CORE VALUES

1. WE APPRECIATE OUR RELATIONSHIP WITH GOD, AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • devoting daily time to reading the Scriptures and thinking on them and praying, which is the basis of a deep personal relationship with God for the knowledge of Him and His will;
  • making an effort to live according to Bible principles and share them with others, inspiring them to read, consider, and practice the Scriptures in their daily lives;
  • personal changes under the influence of the Word of God, the Holy Spirit, and the life example of Christ.

    • 2. WE APPRECIATE THE GLORY AND WORSHIP OF GOD, AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • teaching how to properly worship God in Spirit and Truth;
  • prompt to the true worship of God, which is manifested through character (behavior); dedicated time to glorifying and praising God in prayers, songs, and music; dedicated service to people;
  • organized meetings and services of the church, where people can sincerely glorify God and worship Him through prayer, music, singing, and personal service.

    • 3. WE APPRECIATE THE COMMUNITY (CHURCH), AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • care about the unity of the community, despite the many differences between each other in character, talents, abilities in service;
  • take part in communion for unity with Christ and the Church;
  • build open and sincere relationships through good deeds and attention to each other's needs;
  • take an active part in various meetings, home groups, and services of the church, supporting it with our prayers, gifts, talents, and generous donations.

    • 4. WE APPRECIATE THE TESTIMONY OF JESUS CHRIST, AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • testify about Him to other people with our lives and words;
  • pray pray for missionaries, support them financially and equip church members for the mission;
  • organize organize evangelistic events; social projects; use an online resource to bring the gospel to people.

    • 5. WE APPRECIATE SERVICES ACCORDING TO THE GIFTS OF THE HOLY SPIRIT, AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • we use the service the service of the gifts of the Holy Spirit to bring glory to God, to improve each other, and to build the church and to benefit it;
  • serve and encourage others to serve effectively according to spiritual gifts;
  • focused on achieving the vision and goals of the local Church, not personal ones.

    • 6. WE APPRECIATE THE FAMILY, AND THIS IS MANIFESTED IN:

  • serve to build in our families an atmosphere of love and trust, harmony and acceptance, investing personal time, physical and emotional effort and material resources, etc.;
  • develop family ministry in our church to build a biblical relationship between husband and wife, as well as between parents and children through premarital education; family seminars and conferences; crisis counseling, etc.;
  • disseminate biblical family values in society through participation in social events for supporting family values; family celebrations, seminars, conferences, and personal counseling.
